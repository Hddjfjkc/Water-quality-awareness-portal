# Water Quality Awareness Portal

## Overview

The Water Quality Awareness Portal is a web-based application developed to educate users about water quality, contamination sources, health risks, water safety standards, and purification methods. The portal aims to promote awareness about safe drinking water and encourage healthy water practices.

## Features

* Responsive and user-friendly interface
* Information about water contamination and pollution
* Water quality awareness content
* Details about water purification methods
* Interactive and visually appealing design
* Accessible across desktop and mobile devices

## Technologies Used

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript

## Project Objectives

* Increase public awareness about water quality issues
* Provide educational information regarding water safety
* Promote the importance of clean and safe drinking water
* Encourage communities to adopt better water management practices

## Installation

1. Download or clone the repository.
2. Open the project folder.
3. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.

## Future Enhancements
